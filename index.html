We Are HealEnv

We, at Heal Environment, take such measures that spread awareness among public about a crucial and critical problem: environmental crisis.Our goal is to make communities aware, ensure protection of the environment by developing skills that will help identify and solve problems related to environment.

What We Do

We are raising awareness regarding the causes and effects of Climate change to the masses

Awareness Sessions

We are going to have some advantageous sessions in Azad Kashmir regarding environment. The number of sessions would be approximately 30. We would invite our public and students to attend these sessions.

Plantation Drive

In this duration, our target is to complete fifty plantation drives in Azad Jammu and Kashmir.

Waste Management Drive

The completion of 10 waste drives in big cities would be our another target and we will engage municipality as well.

Promote Green Energy

To spread awareness regarding green energy would be one of main goals. We are full of natural resources such as to produce hydroelectricity. The only need of hour is to aware our public and government.




Latest Updates

The third weekly virtual session of "Environment Does Matter" supported by U.S Mission in Pakistan in Partnership with Pakistan-U.S Alumni Network (PUAN). "Environment Does Matter" a 11-Week Virtual Program supported by U.S Mission in Pakistan in Partnership with Pakistan-U.S Alumni Network (PUAN) held it's third session on Friday, 10th December, 2021, and students of Mirpur University of Science and Technology (MUST) and University of Kotli AJ&K (UOK) participated widely in the virtual session. The topic of the session was "Ecological footprint and Environment Sustainability" We would like to say "Thank you for the good job" to every single participant of the program. A very special "Thank you" to our panelists Mr. Muneeb Ur Rehman(Ex president MUST debating society) and Miss. Bushra Zaman( PhD research scholar Quaid-e-Azam University Islamabad). The program, conducted on Microsoft Team, was headed by Ehtsham Ul Haq Raja (English Works Alumna, 2019) and in first 10 minutes he had discussion with students about what today's session will be about. Then, the session was handed over to the panelists.First, Mr. Muneeb Ur Rehman, while discussing on the given topic, shared with students how to overcome the challenges related to our environment. He also shared with students how they can reduce their carbon footprints. Then, Miss Bushra Zaman shared her knowledge and ideas about the Ecological Footprint and Environment Sustainability. She told students how they can play their role to stabilise Environment. In last 10 minutes students were allowed to ask questions fron speakers and many students asked questions.Then, Mr. Ehtsham Ul Haq Raja ended the webinar with the routine photo session. The third weekly virtual session was a successful session which will be followed by a series of webinars in coming weeks.
